    How to get here

    The QCD Evolution 2022 workshop will be held at the University of Virginia in Charlottesville, Virginia. For those that are flying into VA there are three airports one can use to get to Charlottesville. One of the airports is Dulles International, another is Richmond International, and the local airport is the Charlottesville-Albemarle Regional Airport.

    When coming from Dulles International Airport take the follwing route VA-28 S to I-66 W to US-29 S all the way into Charlottesville. It's approximately a 2 hour drive to go from Dulles Airport to Charlottesville, VA.

    When coming from Richmond International Airport take the following route I-64 W from Richmond all the way into Charlottesville. It's approximately 1.25 hour drive to go from Richmond International Airport to Charlottesville, VA by car.

    • Group of Hadronic Physics

    We would like to suggest to all of the attendees to join the APS topical Group of Hadronic Physics (GHP) which is dedicated to the advancement of research and knowledge related to the studies of the strong interaction. Increased membership in the GHP results directly in more funding for workshops directly focused on hadronic physics.
